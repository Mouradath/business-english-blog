Making a Good First Impression

Your first day at work sets the tone for your professional relationships. These phrases will help you introduce yourself confidently.

Introducing Yourself to Colleagues

Hello, I'm [name]. I've just started as [job title].

Nice to meet you! I'm really looking forward to working here.

I'll be working in the [department] team.

Asking for Help Politely

Could you help me with...?

Sorry to bother you, but could you show me how to...?

I'm not sure where to find... Could you point me in the right direction?

Understanding Office Culture

What time do people usually have lunch?

Is there a dress code here?

What's the best way to contact you — email or Teams?

✓ What You Learned

Be friendly, ask questions, and show enthusiasm. Use polite phrases to ask for help. First impressions in the workplace are about attitude as much as language.
