How to Think in English

Stop translating and start thinking directly in English.

← Back to Miscellaneous

Start with Simple Thoughts

Look around and name objects in English.

Describe what you are doing: "I am making coffee."

Comment on the weather: "It's cloudy today."

Internal Monologue in English

Give yourself instructions: "I need to buy milk."

Plan your day: "This morning I'll work on emails..."

React to things: "That's interesting!"

Label Your Environment

Put sticky notes on objects with their English names.

Change your phone language to English.

✓ What You Learned

Thinking in English develops gradually. Start with simple observations and build to longer thoughts every day.
