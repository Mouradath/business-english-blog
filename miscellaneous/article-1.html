How to Start Learning English Today

Simple, practical steps to begin your English journey.

← Back to Miscellaneous

Step 1: Set a Clear Goal

Be specific: "I want to have a basic conversation in English within 3 months."

Step 2: Learn the Most Common Words First

Focus on the 500 most common English words first.

Use apps like Duolingo or Anki to learn with spaced repetition.

Step 3: Listen Every Day

Listen to English for at least 15 minutes every day.

Start with slow, clear speech: BBC Learning English.

Step 4: Speak from Day One

Don't wait until you're "ready" — start speaking early.

Talk to yourself in English. Describe your surroundings.

✓ What You Learned

Consistency beats intensity — 20 minutes daily is better than 3 hours once a week.
