📖 1. Making Requests

In English, how you ask for something is very important. A direct command like "Do this!" sounds rude. Instead, use modal verbs to make your request sound polite and friendly.

Request PhraseFormality
Can you help me?Informal — between friends
Could you help me?Polite — everyday situations
Would you mind helping me?More formal / careful
Could you possibly help me?Very polite — formal situations
I was wondering if you could help.Very polite / indirect
Would it be possible to...?Formal — work or emails
✅ Real Dialogue

A: Excuse me, could you possibly open the door? My hands are full.
B: Of course! No problem at all.

A: I was wondering if you could send me the report by Friday.
B: Sure, I'll have it ready by Thursday.

💡
Grammar tip: "Would you mind" is always followed by the -ing form of the verb — never the infinitive.
✅ "Would you mind closing the window?"   ❌ "Would you mind close the window?"

📝 2. Responding to Requests

When someone makes a request, you need to either accept it or refuse it politely. A flat "No" without explanation sounds rude — always give a short reason.

✅ Accepting a Request

• Of course! / Sure!
• No problem at all.
• I'd be happy to.
• Certainly! Leave it to me.
• Yes, of course. I'll do it right away.

⚠️ Refusing Politely

• I'm sorry, I'm afraid I can't right now.
• I'd love to, but I'm really busy at the moment.
• I'm sorry, that's not really possible today.
• Unfortunately, I won't be able to — could someone else help?

🎯
Politeness tip: When refusing, always start with "I'm sorry" or "I'm afraid" and give a brief reason. This keeps the conversation friendly even when the answer is no.

⚠️ 3. Common Mistakes

These are errors that A2/B1 learners make most often with requests. Study them carefully!

❌ Common Errors

• ❌ "Would you mind to close the window?" — Wrong: use -ing, not infinitive
• ❌ "Can you possibly to help me?" — Never use "to" after "can"
• ❌ "I want you do this." — Missing "to" after "want"
• ❌ "No." — Too blunt when refusing; always soften it

✅ Correct Forms

• ✅ "Would you mind closing the window?"
• ✅ "Could you possibly help me?"
• ✅ "I want you to do this."
• ✅ "I'm sorry, I'm afraid I can't right now."
