Sharing Your View

Being able to give an opinion clearly is essential in discussions, interviews and everyday conversations.

Giving Your Opinion

In my opinion, ...

I think / I believe / I feel that ...

From my point of view, ...

Personally, I think ...

Agreeing

I completely agree with you.

That's exactly what I think.

You make a good point.

I couldn't agree more.

Disagreeing Politely

I see your point, but I think ...

I'm not sure I agree because ...

That's an interesting view, but ...

I understand what you mean, however ...

✓ What You Learned

Always soften disagreements with "I see your point, but..." This keeps conversations positive and respectful while still allowing you to share your honest opinion.
