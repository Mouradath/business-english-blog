📖 1. Personality Adjectives

In English, we use adjectives to describe what someone is like. These words go after the verb "be" or before a noun.

PositiveMeaningNegativeMeaning
kindcaring, helpfulunkindnot caring
generousgives freelyselfishthinks only of self
patientcalm, not rushedimpatienteasily frustrated
reliablecan be trustedunreliablecannot be trusted
ambitiouswants to succeedlazyavoids effort
sociableenjoys companyshyuncomfortable socially
determinednever gives upstubbornrefuses to change
✅ Using Personality Adjectives

• She is very kind. She always helps her friends.
• He is ambitious. He wants to become a doctor.
• They are sociable. They love meeting new people.
• My boss is reliable. She always keeps her promises.

💡
Grammar tip: Personality adjectives go after the verb "be": "She is kind." They also go before a noun: "She is a kind person." Both are correct!

📝 2. Describing Physical Appearance

We also use adjectives to describe how someone looks. In English, there is a specific order for appearance adjectives.

✅ Appearance Vocabulary

Height: tall, short, medium height
Build: slim, thin, well-built, stocky
Hair: long, short, curly, straight, dark, blonde, grey
Eyes: blue, brown, green, dark
Age: young, middle-aged, elderly

✅ Full Description Example

"My friend Maria is tall and slim. She has long, curly dark hair and brown eyes. She's in her thirties. She's also very friendly and outgoing."

🎯
Natural order: When describing someone, English speakers usually go from general to specific: height → build → hair → eyes → personality. Following this order makes your descriptions sound natural.

⚠️ 3. Common Mistakes

These are the most common errors beginners make when describing people.

❌ Common Errors

• ❌ "She is a tall hair woman." — wrong adjective order
• ❌ "He is very ambition." — "ambition" is a noun, not an adjective
• ❌ "She has got blond hairs." — "hair" is uncountable in English
• ❌ "He is very much kind." — don't use "much" with adjectives

✅ Correct Forms

• ✅ "She is a tall woman with dark hair."
• ✅ "He is very ambitious."
• ✅ "She has blonde hair."
• ✅ "He is very kind."
