Common Writing Mistakes Even Fluent Speakers Make

Polish your English writing by avoiding subtle but important errors.

← Back to Writing

Comma Splices

❌ I finished the report, it took three hours.

✔ I finished the report. It took three hours.

✔ I finished the report, and it took three hours.

Dangling Modifiers

❌ Walking to school, the rain started.

✔ Walking to school, I noticed the rain start.

That vs Which

That = essential (no comma): The book that I recommended is great.

Which = extra info (with comma): The book, which I loved, is now out of print.

✓ What You Learned

Avoid comma splices, dangling modifiers, and confusion between that/which. Editing with these in mind will significantly raise your writing quality.
