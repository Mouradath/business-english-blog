Writing Short Paragraphs in English

Learn how to group sentences into a clear and focused paragraph.

← Back to Writing

What Is a Paragraph?

A paragraph is a group of sentences about one main idea. A good paragraph has a clear beginning, middle and end.

The Three Parts of a Paragraph

1. Topic sentence — states the main idea

2. Supporting sentences — explain or give examples

3. Closing sentence — summarises or concludes

Example Paragraph

Topic: My favourite season

[Topic] My favourite season is autumn.

[Support] I love the orange and red colours of the leaves.

[Support] The weather is cool but not too cold.

[Closing] For these reasons, autumn is the perfect season for me.

✓ What You Learned

Every paragraph needs a topic sentence, supporting sentences and a closing sentence. One paragraph = one main idea. This makes your writing clear and easy to read.
